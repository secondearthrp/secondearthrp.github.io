  • Roleplay

    • Guidelines

      • AI-generated content

        • Can I use AI-generated content?

          • You're welcome to use AI-generated images as your OC's avatar in Second Earth, but please be honest and open about this, and don't claim AI-generated images (or anyone else's, for that matter) as your own artwork, or try to earn money from them.
          • Although strongly discouraged, you can use AI-generated text to help you plan and phrase your roleplay speech and actions, but again, please be transparent with us about it if you do, and try not to rely on it too heavily.
          • AI should be used as a tool; a walking stick rather than a wheelchair. We can almost certainly tell when a robot has written text or generated images.

    • Government

      • Diplomacy

        • Nation alliances

          • What are nation alliances?

            • A nation alliance is a group of two or more nations affiliated with one another.
            • Members of an alliance might share resources, or offer each other military and/or financial help in wars or after disasters.

          • How are nation alliances formed?

            • Nation alliances are formed in the #diplomacy channel, usually in a thread.
            • Alliances are usually organised by world leaders or other high-ranking members of the government, who may then make a statement in the #leader-statements channel.

      • Leader statements

        • What warrants a leader statement?

          • World leaders will usually make speeches and announcements in response to world events, like wars, alliance formations and natural disasters.
          • Leader statements are generally long-form posts, often include an attached image, and are often frequent in nature, much like statements from real-life world leaders.

        • Where are leader statements made?

          • Leader statements are made in the #leader-statements channel.

    • Time

      • What time is it on Second Earth?

        • The server time can be viewed on the clock page.

      • How does time on Second Earth work?

        • Time in Second Earth is based on the real Unix time, starting on March 10, 2124, and progresses about 4 times faster than real time.
        • This means that one real-life week is approximately one Second Earth month, and one real-life second is a little over four Second Earth seconds.

  • Server

    • Boosting

      • What perks do I get for Boosting the server?

        • You can unlock a number of neat perks by Boosting Second Earth. You get a hoisted Booster role and a custom colour role, permission to add an emoji of your choice, and you get to help out with testing shiny new features.
        • To avoid making things pay-to-win, Boosting our server will not provide you any advantages in the RP.
