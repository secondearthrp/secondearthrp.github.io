Second Earth Rules & Guidelines.

First, thank you for taking the time to read the server guidelines. These rules have been put in place for good reason, and encourage every member of the server to have a positive experience in both the RP, and the server itself.

Please keep in mind that every incident is dealt with on a case-by-case basis, and not necessarily by concrete rules.

Server Guidelines

These rules are in relation to the server itself. These rules apply in all channels, both roleplay and out-of-character.

  1. Treat others with respect.

    • Keep discussions and roleplay civil.

    • Bullying and harassment is never welcome, and is taken very seriously.

    • Discrimination of any kind is not tolerated. Hate speech and slurs will be filtered and result in a ban.

  2. No explicit NSFW content.

    • This includes pornographic, violent, gory, or otherwise explicit content.

    • This covers all types of media (video, text, images, etc.).

  3. No unwarranted spam or advertising.

    • Unnecessary or irrelevant advertising for any external source or server is treated as spam.

    • This includes messages, reactions and @mentions.

    • DM advertising is prohibited by Discord's Terms of Service, and will therefore result in a ban from the server. Please report any members who violate this rule.

  4. No impersonation.

    • Please do not impersonate, act as, or pretend to be any other server member, even if given permission by that member.

    • This goes for all users, including members, bots, staff members and Timekeeper.

    • This also applies to Tupperbox: do not use Tupperbox to pretend to be someone else, nor pretend to be someone else's Tupperbox OC.

  5. Please only speak English in all channels.

    • Phrases in other languages are allowed, provided they are both relevant and easy to understand in context (e.g. "Vivre la France", "Presidente d'Italia").

    • If you must speak in another language, please provide an accurate English translation in, before or after the message.

    • English is the easiest language for us to moderate, and is also the language the vast majority of our members speak.

  6. Follow Discord's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.

    • This should go without saying, but you must follow Discord's rules and guidelines at all times.

Roleplay Guidelines

These rules are in relation to roleplay. These rules apply in roleplay channels and scenarios.

  1. No godmoding.

    • Do not make your characters invulnerable, omnipotent or otherwise overpowered.

    • Your OC should not be able to know information that is not already disclosed or known to them.

    • Each of your OCs are independent. One OC may know something that another does not.

  2. No powerplaying.

    • Powerplaying is the act of controlling or making another person's character do something.

    • You may only control your own characters.

  3. No real-world geopolitics.

    • Try to avoid recreating real-world political events (the Ukraine-Russia war, the Israeli-Gaza war, etc.).

  4. No real-world extremist ideologies or organisations.

    • Avoid recreating real-world extremist ideologies or organisations (Nazism, ISIS, etc.).

  5. No NSFW scenes.

    • Implied "intimacy" is allowed, but explicit details are not. If your RP somehow comes to this, fade to black (e.g. "the pair entered the room and close the door firmly behind them...").

  6. Do not play Mary Sue or Gary Stu.

    • Mary Sue (or Gary Stu) are character archetypes that have unbound strengths and no weaknesses.

    • Every character's strengths and weaknesses must be relatively balanced.

  7. Do not kill or permanently harm other people's characters without consent.

    • You are only allowed to kill off, disable or disfigure an OC if it is either your own or you have permission from the OC's owner to do so.

  8. Include as much detail in your RP as possible.

    • Aim to make your RP message about a paragraph long. Brief interactions can be kept to a sentence or so; about 10 to 20 words is okay.

    • We put a lot of effort into building this fictional world, and it can be difficult to build off and reply to one-liner messages.

  9. Weapons of mass destruction are given only under very special circumstances.

    • Biological and nuclear weapons must be approved by a member of staff, and only used on a small scale for good reason.

  10. Roleplay channels are for roleplay only.

    • When typing in a roleplay channel, you must stay in character. There is an out-of-character (OOC) channel you can use to discuss roleplays. Otherwise, use the general channel.

  11. No AI-generated roleplay.

    • We ask that you do not use AI-generated text in your roleplay.

    • You may use AI for inspiration to help plan and phrase your roleplay, but please do not use raw AI-generated text.

    • AI-generated images and OC avatars are permitted, as long as you remain honest and open about it.

    • It is extremely easy to tell when content is synthetic, and so we ask that you don't attempt to lie about it. There is no reason to do so, nor is there any shame in having to use AI-generated imagery.

  12. No violating the laws of physics.

    • The server might be sci-fi, but we want to keep things relatively within the realm of possibility.

    • This includes but is not limited to infinite energy systems and perpetual motion machines.
